# FLUX Prompt Kit · site2026 Indian craft asset system

Every prompt shares one style DNA so assets feel like a set. Append the
**style suffix** below to each prompt. Generate on the plain khadi
background so cutouts are one magic-wand away.

**Style suffix (append to every prompt):**

> muted Indian craft palette of vat indigo #0e1220, unbleached khadi
> #ece2cc, madder red #8f3b2e, turmeric gold #d9a441 and peacock teal
> #1f5f5b, natural-dye blockprint aesthetic, soft directional light,
> matte handmade texture, minimalist negative space, photographed like a
> still-life catalogue asset

**Cutout rule:** for assets, add *"single object centered on a plain
solid warm cream background, no shadows touching the edges, product
photography framing"*.

---

## 1. Cutout assets

- **Lotus printing block**: a carved teak printing block with a lotus
  face up, crisp chisel marks, beside its madder-red inked impression on
  khadi cloth
- **Paisley block**: a worn wooden paisley printing block, handle
  polished smooth by use, faint turmeric pigment in the grooves
- **Brass diya**: a small hand-beaten brass diya with a lit cotton wick,
  one warm flame, faint oil sheen in the bowl
- **Uttarayan kite**: a paper fighter kite in madder and turmeric panels
  with a bamboo spine, beside a wooden spool of glass-coated line
- **Peacock feather**: a single peacock feather with a deep teal eye,
  gentle curve, barbs slightly separated
- **Indigo cloth bundle**: a bundle of freshly indigo-dyed cloth
  mid-unfold, gradient from deep vat blue to sky where the dye thinned
- **Marigold garland strand**: a short strand of stitched marigolds,
  saffron and amber heads, cotton thread visible
- **Powder rangoli**: a small rangoli of colored powder, six-fold
  geometry in madder, turmeric and teal, edges soft where fingers pressed
