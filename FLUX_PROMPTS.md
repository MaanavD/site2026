# FLUX Prompt Kit — site2026 Japandi asset system

Every prompt shares one style DNA so assets feel like a set. Append the
**style suffix** below to each prompt. Generate on the plain kinari
background so cutouts are one magic-wand away.

**Style suffix (append to every prompt):**

> muted Japandi palette of charcoal black #16161c, warm kinari paper #eae3d2,
> deep pine green #34503e and pale moss #a3bfa8, sumi-e ink wash
> aesthetic, soft directional light, matte texture, minimalist negative
> space, photographed like a still-life catalogue asset

**Cutout rule:** for assets, add — *"single object centered on a plain
solid warm cream background, no shadows touching the edges, product
photography framing"*.

---

## 1. Cutout assets (the "assets of sorts" direction)

- **Ensō circle** — a single bold ensō circle painted in one sumi ink
  brushstroke, dry-brush texture at the tail, slightly imperfect and open
  at the top right
- **Paper lantern** — a weathered washi paper lantern glowing warm amber
  from within, faint bamboo ribs visible
- **Pine branch** — a lone Japanese black pine branch with dense needle
  clusters, bonsai-styled, slight downward sweep
- **Stone stack** — three smooth river stones stacked in decreasing size,
  charcoal grey with subtle mineral veining
- **Calligraphy brush** — a well-used calligraphy brush with dark ink
  still wet on the bristles, bamboo handle, resting at a slight angle
- **Tea bowl** — a wabi-sabi raku tea bowl with kintsugi gold repair
  lines, dark glaze with persimmon undertones
- **Origami crane** — a single origami crane folded from kinari paper
  with faint deckled texture
- **Hanko seal** — a square vermillion hanko stamp block, carved base
  visible, beside a small tin of red seal paste

## 2. Hero / atmosphere (if you ever swap the WebGL shader for stills or video)

- black ink dispersing in water against darkness, billowing smoke-like
  plumes catching a single warm amber light source, ultra slow motion
  feel, cinematic macro
- dawn mist drifting through a dense black forest of pine, only
  silhouettes visible, one distant warm lantern glow between the trunks
- extreme close-up of handmade washi paper fibers, raking warm light,
  charcoal ink bleeding into the sheet from the left edge

## 3. Work card covers (1200×800, consistent series)

- **FLUX / BFL** — an ensō circle mid-stroke where the ink transforms
  into a flowing stream of tiny latent-noise pixels, dark background
- **Foundry Local** — a miniature charcoal foundry crucible pouring
  glowing amber light instead of metal onto a paper surface
- **ONNX Runtime** — a zen garden of raked sand where the rake lines form
  a neural network graph, one persimmon stone as a node
- **Hush** — a paper lantern with sound waves rendered as concentric ink
  rings frozen around it, absolute stillness

## 4. Blog covers (template)

> a minimal ikebana arrangement interpreting "{POST THEME}", single

## 5. Video idea (hero or /about b-roll)
